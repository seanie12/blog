## Theorem 2.1.3 (Triangular inequality)
For all $x,y\in \mathbb{R}, |x+y|\leq |x|+|y|$

<*proof*>

$$
\begin{align*}
0\leq (x+y)^2 &=x^2+2xy + y^2 \\
&\leq |x|^2 + 2|x|\cdot |y| + |y|^2 =(|x|+|y|)^2
\end{align*}
$$

Thus, 

$$
\begin{align*}
|x+y| = \sqrt{(x+y)^2} \leq \sqrt{(|x|+|y|)^2} = |x| +|y|
\end{align*}
$$

$$\tag*{$\square$}$$
